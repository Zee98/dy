Pioneers Debate competition (PDC)

Debates can help students practice and demonstrate their critical thinking skills and cultivate their persuasion and communication skills. Also debate provides experiences that are conductive to life-changing, cognitive and presentation skills.

Pioneers Debate Competition (#PDC) is three (3) days British parliamentary debate program aligned with the Sustainable Development of Goals (SDGs), Youth Development and Women Empowerment with more than 80 university and school students (debaters) in Kabul, Afghanistan which will be held by Dynamic Youth Afghanistan twice a year in collaboration with some contributing organizations.

This program aims to enhance soft skills of Afghan Young leaders. Our debate competition is a unique chance for Afghans to speak up their minds and work on the possible solutions for the local, regional and global level issues. This program aims to increase the learners and debater’s confidence, poise, and self-esteem. We are providing an engaging learner, solution centered activities, active and experiential learning methodology.

This program aims to increase the learners and debater’s confidence, poise, and self-esteem. PDC is designed for:
  • Boosting teamwork & cooperation ability.
  • Building self-confidence & public speaking skills.
  • Consolidating the analysis skills.
  • Enriching open-mindedness.
  • Developing leadership, team building and community skills.
  • Developing effective speech composition and delivery.
  • Training critical thinking skills.
  • Improving learners’ ability to form balanced, informed arguments and to use reasoning and evidence.

Through PDC, which is the biggest debating competition among University and high school students ever, we help Afghan youth to become “better thinkers, speakers and leaders”.

Them: Think, Speak, Impact.

Program requirement:
  • At least intermediate level of English language
  • Currently School/ University Students
  • Students who interests in Sustainable Development Goals
  • 0+ Youth Leaders from across the country
  • Committed to attend all the sessions.
